# Elarion — The Luminous Realm

Elarion drifts between the fabric of dreams and energy. Every spark of light carries a thought, a whisper of memory. The forests rise like cathedrals of glass, their trunks pulsing with slow, radiant heartbeats. Rivers of liquid brightness weave through the land, singing soft harmonic tones that guide travelers through the endless crystalline canopy.

## Inhabitants

The **Auryn** are beings woven from color and resonance. They speak in waves of light, weaving meaning through synchronized hues. Harmony has ruled for ages.

## The Red Eclipse

Until the *Red Eclipse* fractured the spectrum—creating the **Shadow Groves**, zones where light bends wrongly and consciousness fades. From these regions, dark mimicries of the Auryn emerged—silent, reflective, yearning for the brilliance they lost.

## The First After

Yet hope remains. The first Auryn born after the eclipse carries a shard of pure spectrum within its chest, glowing in all colors at once. Many believe it heralds the *Reunification of Light*—a new dawn for Elarion.
